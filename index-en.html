Digital Bookkeeping

TallyKhata empowers small shops and businesses to record and manage daily sales, purchases, stock, expenses, and notes. An easy to use all-in-one app keeps businesses organized.

Digital Wallet

TallyPay, digital wallet of TallyKhata, is licensed by central bank of Bangladesh. It offers QR payment, instant money out, add money, savings, mobile recharge etc.

Digital Payment

TallyPay QR is an interoperable payment system that enables businesses to accept payments from any MFS or bank app. It is designed for shops and small to large enterprises.

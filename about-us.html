Company Overview

About Progoti Systems

Progoti Systems Limited is a Bangladesh-based financial technology company founded in 2010, focused on digital financial services, small business digitisation, and scalable payment platforms. Its flagship platform TallyKhata serves ~1 million monthly active small merchants with digital bookkeeping, payments, and financial data. TallyPay, its licensed payment arm, enables QR-based digital wallet and merchant payment services. Through SureCash, the company partnered with five banks to deliver mobile financial services and Bangladesh's largest digital government stipend disbursement — covering ~10 million primary school mothers.

Bangladesh's largest digital bookkeeping platform for small merchants. Serves ~1M monthly active users with digital records, customer balances, and business insights.

Licensed Payment Service

Digital wallet and payment platform under Bangladesh Bank PSP Licence. Enables QR payments, digital wallets, bank linking, and national payment infrastructure connectivity.

  • Bangla QR payment acceptance
  • eKYC digital wallet onboarding
  • Bank account integration
  • Interoperable payment network

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a leading digital financial technology company that empowers small businesses, citizens, institutions, and government programmes through inclusive, scalable, and secure digital platforms.

Mission

To build simple, reliable, and accessible digital tools that help small businesses manage operations, accept payments, access financial services, and participate in the formal digital economy.
